Peanut Butter Bar Recipe

Description

This no-bake peanut butter bar recipe is a quick and easy treat that combines the rich flavors of peanut butter and chocolate.

This recipe will serve 12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix peanut butter and honey until smooth.
  2. Add rolled oats, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well combined.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Press the mixture into a greased 8x8 inch baking dish.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour until firm.
  6. Cut into bars and serve.

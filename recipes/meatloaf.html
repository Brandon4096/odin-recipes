Meatloaf Recipe

Description

This meatloaf recipe is a classic comfort food that combines ground beef, breadcrumbs, and seasonings for a hearty meal.

This recipe will serve 6

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup ketchup (for topping)

  4. Spread 1/2 cup of ketchup on top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 160째F (70째C).
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
